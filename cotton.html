Crop Information

Cotton is a soft, fluffy fiber that grows in a boll around the seeds of the cotton plant. It is used mainly in the textile industry.

Common Diseases

  • Wilt Disease
  • Root Rot
  • Leaf Curl Virus

Suitable Fertilizers

  • Nitrogen (Urea)
  • Phosphorus (Superphosphate)
  • Potassium (Muriate of Potash)
